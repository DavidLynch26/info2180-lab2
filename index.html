Beserk

September 28, 2022 by David

Beserk is an original manga written by Kentaro Miura which started in the year of 1989 and has continued to present even with the unfortunate death of the author in 2021. The manga follows the protagonist by the name of "Guts" which was given by the guesome manner in which he was born. The story follows this twisted hero on his journey fighting mercanaries until it twists into a spiral of betrayal and our hero turns into what he tried his best to get rid of. He starts going after strenght and power by any means to take down the friend that turned foe.

Thought the author has met his end at the young age of 54, his legacy lives on as the series continues in his absence.

Jujutsu Kaisen

June 15, 2022 by David

Jujutsu Jujutsu Kaisen is a fairly new anime which was released in October of 2020. It's creator Gege Akutami got a quick start on the animation as the manga was released closely before in december of the 2019. this series follows our main character Yuji Itadori, a high schooler with abnormal strenght and speed in comparison to his peers. This aids in his efforts members of the club he joined, the "Occult Research Club" are attacked by monters known as "cursed spirits" which take counless lives per year.

The story follows Itadori as he becomes a jujutsu sorcerers on his journey to get stronger and cleanse cursed spirits while attending Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College with sorcerers of differing skillsets.
